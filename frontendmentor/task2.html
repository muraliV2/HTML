Simple omletter receipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total : approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation : 5 minutes
  • Cooking : 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan Place a non-stick grying pan over medium heat and butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omlette Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs and tit the pan
  4. Fold and serveAs the omlette continues to cook,Carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.
  5. EnjoyServe hot,with additional salt and pepper,if needed.
